Why Your Support Matters

Your donation directly funds the education of 600+ Muslim girls who would otherwise lack access to quality Islamic education. The Jamia operates in one of India's most educationally backward districts, where the female literacy rate is only 52.17% — well below the national average.

The institution relies entirely on the generosity of philanthropists and well-wishers. Every rupee you donate goes directly toward teacher salaries, accommodation, meals, books, and infrastructure for our resident students.
