About the Institution

❋

Jamia Nooria Gulshan-e-Zahra was established in March 2015 in just 6 months. It is located in Sibrampur, Uttar Dinajpur — a socially backward, predominantly rural district where the female literacy rate stands at approximately 52.17%, well below the national average.

The Jamia was founded to address this gap and provide quality Islamic and modern education to Muslim girls under proper Islamic guidelines. Today, over 600 full-time girl students are enrolled, taught by 26 expert female teachers day and night.

Operating under the blessed guidance of Ahle Sunnat Wa Jama'at (Maslak-e-Aala Hazrat), the institution stands as a beacon of Islamic knowledge and female empowerment in the Seemaanchal region of West Bengal.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to ensure self-reliance of its Muslim girl students resulting in the well-being and progress of their families. To bring about social reformation and develop human resources through the empowerment of marginalized groups thereby contributing to the overall progress of the society.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be the leading institution for Islamic female education in Seemaanchal, producing well-rounded, knowledgeable women who embody the values of Ahle Sunnat Wa Jama'at and contribute positively to society.
