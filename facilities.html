Campus Overview

✦ ❋ ✦

The campus spans 35,000 sq. ft. of built-up area, providing a complete, self-sufficient environment for over 600 resident students. Every facility has been designed to ensure a safe, nurturing, and academically stimulating environment in keeping with Islamic values.

Campus Facilities

Everything needed for a self-sufficient residential Islamic education

Darul Ifta

Islamic legal advisory department issuing Fatwas. A centre for Islamic jurisprudence serving the broader Muslim community.

Tajushshariah Computer Centre

Dedicated computer lab for digital literacy, desktop publishing, and modern skills training for students.

Library

Extensive collection of Islamic and academic books — from classical Hadith compilations to modern reference materials.

Ladies Masjid

A dedicated prayer hall exclusively for students and staff, fostering a spiritually enriching daily routine.

Girls Hostel

22 rooms providing safe and comfortable accommodation for 600+ resident students from across the region.

Dining Hall & Kitchen

Nutritious meals served daily to all resident students, prepared in a hygienic, fully-equipped kitchen facility.

Seminar Hall

4 large halls for academic events, lectures, graduations, and community programs hosted by the Jamia.

Guest Room

Comfortable accommodation for visiting scholars, educators, and distinguished guests of the institution.

Tailoring Training Centre

Vocational skill development in tailoring and embroidery — equipping students with livelihood skills.

Medical Care

On-campus health facilities ensuring the physical well-being of all 600+ resident students and staff.

Water Filtration Plant

RO (Reverse Osmosis) plant providing clean, safe drinking water to all residents throughout the year.

Electricity Generator

Backup generator ensuring uninterrupted power supply for all academic and residential activities.

24-Hour Security

Round-the-clock night guard providing a safe and secure environment for all students and staff.

Ablution Place

Dedicated Wudhu (ablution) facilities for students and staff to maintain ritual purity for prayers.

Playground

Recreation area providing students with healthy physical activity and relaxation between studies.

Future Plans

Our vision for expanding the Jamia's impact on the community

English Medium School

A CBSE board-affiliated English medium school to provide modern education alongside Islamic values.

Nursing Home

A dedicated healthcare facility to serve the medical needs of the local community in this underserved region.

Research & Publication House

A centre for Islamic research and publishing — producing scholarly works in Arabic, Urdu, and Bangla.
