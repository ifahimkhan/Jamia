About the Institution

❋

Jamia Nooria Gulshan-e-Zahra was established in March 2015 in Sibrampur, Uttar Dinajpur — a socially backward district where the female literacy rate stands at approximately 52.17%, well below the national average.

The Jamia was founded to provide quality Islamic and modern education to Muslim girls under proper Islamic guidelines. Today, over 600 full-time girl students are enrolled, taught by 26 expert female teachers day and night.

Operating under the blessed guidance of Ahle Sunnat Wa Jama'at (Maslak-e-Aala Hazrat), the institution stands as a beacon of Islamic knowledge and female empowerment in the Seemaanchal region of West Bengal.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To ensure self-reliance of Muslim girl students resulting in the well-being and progress of their families — bringing social reformation through the empowerment of marginalized communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading institution for Islamic female education in Seemaanchal, producing well-rounded, knowledgeable women who embody the values of Ahle Sunnat Wa Jama'at.
